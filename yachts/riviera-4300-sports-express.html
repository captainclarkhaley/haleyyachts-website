Riviera 4300 Sports Express

A versatile luxury motor yacht built for fun on the water - three decks for entertaining, open-stern access, and overnight comfort for five.

About the 4300 Sports Express

Riviera's 4300 Sports Express is a highly versatile luxury motor yacht with a great sense of fun. You get so much more with the Sports Express. A sports deck for lounging, dining, and entertaining, a sun deck for relaxing at anchor and a sea deck for all your family water sports. If you want to stay overnight or longer the living and accommodation deck comfortably sleeps five adults with a private master stateroom, aft cabin, lavish bathroom with separate shower, main galley and TV lounge.

As the name suggests, the Sports Express will propel you to your favorite spot at over 30 knots, effortlessly and economically. And it's here the 4300 excels. When you arrive, the entire stern of the yacht is free and open to allow exceptional access to the water; for swimming, snorkeling, paddle boarding, fishing or simply relaxing at the water's edge.

Model, layout, and design credited to Riviera.

Model Features

A closer look at how the 4300 Sports Express is laid out, deck by deck.

Sea Deck

An innovative, articulated design transforms the boarding platform into your own private sea deck complete with a step that doubles as a seat when the platform is partly submerged - perfect for children, and all water sports.

BBQ Centre

By integrating the BBQ on the transom, Riviera's design group have included solid surface BBQ and bench-tops, sink and storage further transforming the large boarding platform into a versatile area ideal for relaxed family days or entertaining friends.

Sports Deck

Offers more for entertaining and relaxation; a quality sound system, bar and meals centre with a drawer fridge, icemaker, large sink and solid surface benchtop. A portside door offers quick and easy access to the side deck and forward, or to invite fresh air to flow through at anchor.

Sun Deck

At anchor, this area with a triple sunbed will be in demand. Thoughtful additions include drink holders, a table that folds out from under the forward-facing bow lounge. There's a cool box for drinks, stereo speakers, USB charge points and the option of a fold away euro style awning.

Helm Deck

This is a state-of-the-art command station featuring touch screen navigation and operation technology all designed to make your boating even easier and more pleasurable. Captain and companion seats are adjustable and deeply comfortable.

Accommodation Deck

A beautiful living space filled with natural light. Enjoy all the comforts including a well-appointed galley, sumptuous L-shaped TV lounge, spacious bathroom with separate full-sized shower, private-entry master stateroom and aft cabin with a double berth and king single.

Features and imagery courtesy of Riviera.

Technical Features

The engineering and systems behind the ride.

Blue-Water Hull Design

Created by the international Riviera design team to deliver a soft, dry and sure-footed ride. The ocean-tested structural strength of a Riviera hull will inspire great confidence.

Performance

Class-leading power-to-weight ratio, enhanced torque, impressive fuel efficiency and exhilarating performance is delivered by twin Volvo Penta turbo diesels and individually steerable drives with forward-facing, twin counter-rotating propellers.

Joystick Control

Gives you total command of your yacht and simplifies boat handling particularly docking and maneuvering in tight quarters. Integrating steering, gear shifting and throttle control into a single joystick.

Volvo Penta Glass Cockpit

This fully integrated system combines and streamlines navigation, engine data and other vessel information to customizable displays at the helm making your boating easier and more enjoyable.

CZone Digital Switching

The intelligent power management system that is easy to use and centralizes control and monitoring of all onboard electrical systems from fixed touchscreens or optional iPad.

Sentinel Monitoring

Enables you via the Sentinel Marine App to monitor a range of critical information regarding your Riviera, to receive notifications about events onboard and remotely operate selected CZone circuits and modes.

Solar Power

Using advanced nano-composite materials, the panels are just 2 mm thick and up to 85% lighter than conventional systems. Bonded directly to the hardtop as a 'solar skin' and tailored to each specific model, they deliver up to 90% more coverage and 120% more power output than typical add-on systems.

What Makes Her Work

The 4300 Sports Express is built around three entertaining zones and easy water access, with real overnight comfort below. Design credited to Riviera.

Three Decks, Three Moods

A sports deck for lounging, dining, and entertaining. A sun deck for relaxing at anchor. A sea deck for the family water sports. One boat that changes character as the day does, so the crowd never outgrows the cockpit.

Open-Stern Water Access

The open stern and sea deck put the water right at your feet. Getting in for a swim, loading the tender, or landing the kids' toys is the easy part, the way it ought to be on a boat built for a day out.

Comfort to Stay Aboard

Below, she sleeps five adults: a private master stateroom, an aft cabin, a full bathroom with a separate shower, the main galley, and a TV lounge. Enough boat to turn the day trip into a weekend without anyone roughing it.
