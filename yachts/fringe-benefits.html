About Fringe Benefits

Fringe Benefits is a 2020 Riviera 545 SUV configured for owners who want one boat that does everything: a full-beam master and three staterooms below, a generous saloon and galley up top, and a cockpit built for fishing, diving, swim platform fun, and long lunches at anchor.

Twin Volvo Penta D-11 diesels with approximately 1,000 hours give her real cruising range with the efficiency Riviera SUVs are known for. She lies in West Palm Beach, ready to show.
