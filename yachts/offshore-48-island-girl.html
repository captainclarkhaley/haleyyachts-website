About Island Girl

Island Girl is a classic 48-foot Offshore Sedan, and she offers exactly what has kept Offshore Yachts well respected among experienced cruisers: a comfortable ride, a practical layout, and quality construction. Satin-finished teak throughout, teak and holly sole, galley up, and a sensible two-stateroom, two-head arrangement. She suits an owner-operator who wants a capable cruising motor yacht with real visibility from the helm and accommodations you can actually live in.

Her semi-displacement hull with a partial keel and a wide 15'6" beam give her a solid platform underway. The spacious salon, forward master, port guest cabin, and flybridge under a bimini make her as good for a weekend aboard as for extended coastal cruising. Full walk-around covered side decks with high bulwarks mean you can move around her safely with the weather up.

Power is twin Caterpillar 3126-TA diesels at 420 hp each, installed in 2006, with roughly 2,000 hours. She has been kept up: new batteries throughout and a new Victron inverter/charger in 2026, an 8kw Northern Lights generator, a Sleipner bow thruster, and a full Garmin electronics package. She lies in Palm Beach Gardens and shows well.
