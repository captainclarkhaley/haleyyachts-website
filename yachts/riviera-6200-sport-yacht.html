Riviera 6200 Sport Yacht

Riviera's newest flagship sport yacht. At 68 ft 5 in, the 6200 carries a full-beam master, an alfresco aft galley, and a tender garage, with twin Volvo Penta IPS to move it all.

About the 6200 Sport Yacht

The 6200 Sport Yacht is the next benchmark in the Platinum Edition range, and it makes its world premiere in 2026. Riviera's new-generation hull lets this boat give you far more than its length suggests: three deck zones that flow into one another, a full-beam master amidships, and the volume to host a crowd by day and sleep eight to nine in genuine comfort by night.

The story of the 6200 is the aft deck. An all-seasons space with an integrated alfresco galley sits right at the social heart of the yacht, opening to the cockpit and the water beyond. Forward, a glorious foredeck with twin sunbeds gives you a second living room in the open air. Below, a gourmet galley, three saloon lounges, and a second dining setting keep everyone comfortable whatever the weather.

Model Features

A closer look at how the 6200 Sport Yacht is laid out, deck by deck.

Aft Deck and Alfresco Galley

An all-seasons aft deck with an integrated alfresco galley sits at the social heart of the boat. Cook, serve, and gather without leaving the party, with the cockpit and the water just a step away. It is the space that defines how the 6200 is meant to be lived in.

Foredeck Retreat

Forward sunbeds turn the glorious foredeck into a second open-air lounge. It is the quiet end of the boat at anchor, away from the cockpit crowd, with the best seat in the house for sunsets.

Saloon and Dining

Three sumptuous saloon lounges and a second dining setting with a fold-away table give you room to spread out indoors. The whole space is filled with light and flows straight out to the aft deck when the weather is right.

Gourmet Galley

A gourmet-level galley with premium equipment handles real cooking for a full guest list, not just snacks underway. An optional wet bar adds wine cooling and glassware storage for the evenings.

Full-Beam Master Suite

The full-beam master amidships is the kind of owner's space you expect on a much larger yacht: the widest part of the hull given over entirely to you, with an ensuite and natural light from both sides.

Guest Accommodation

A forward VIP stateroom and port and starboard guest staterooms sleep eight to nine across three to four cabins, with three ensuite bathrooms. An optional lower lounge or crew cabin with a Pullman berth adapts the layout to how you cruise.

Tender Garage and Swim Platform

A dedicated tender garage with a roller launch system swallows the dinghy and the toys, kayaks, paddleboards, and Sea Bobs, so the decks stay clear. The two-tiered swim platform makes getting in and out of the water effortless.

Features and imagery courtesy of Riviera.

Technical Features

The engineering and systems behind the ride.

New-Generation Blue-Water Hull

The 6200 rides on Riviera's new-generation hull, engineered for a soft, dry, sure-footed ride. It is what lets a boat this size carry the volume it does while still handling like a sport yacht should.

Twin Volvo Penta IPS

Twin Volvo Penta D13 IPS pods, in 900 hp or 1,000 hp tunes, deliver strong torque, efficient cruising, and forward-facing counter-rotating propellers. The result is the easy, refined performance an owner-operator wants on a 68-footer.

Joystick Control and Glass Cockpit

Joystick control turns docking a flagship into a one-person job, integrating steering, shifting, and throttle. The Volvo Penta Glass Cockpit streamlines navigation and engine data into clean, customizable displays at the helm.

CZone, Sentinel, and Lithium Power

CZone digital switching centralizes control of every onboard electrical system, and Sentinel monitoring lets you check on the boat and operate selected circuits from your phone. Maintenance-free lithium-ion batteries handle house power, with an optional solar skin to extend time at anchor.
