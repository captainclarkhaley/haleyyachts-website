About Fortunato

Fortunato is a 1991 Southern Wind 72, built to a Bruce Farr Fast 72 design at the South African yard whose reputation for build quality has shaped the high-performance cruising category ever since. She is equally at home crossing oceans, effortlessly clicking off 200-mile days, or pointing high in a long-distance offshore race.

This is a sailor's boat. The kind of hull you take to the Caribbean for a season and then keep going. Farr's design pedigree means she rewards a competent crew with real speed under sail, and the Southern Wind build means she is engineered to take it. For an owner who has been waiting for a performance 72 at a serious-value asking, Fortunato is worth the showing.
