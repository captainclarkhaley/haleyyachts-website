Services

Consultative guidance through every stage of yacht ownership - buying or selling.

Buying Assistance

Finding the right boat starts with a conversation - not a questionnaire. I want to hear about your boating history, what gets you excited about being on the water, your budget, your timeline, and how you plan to use the boat. So much of this process is fluid and emotional, and I'd rather get to know you than check boxes.

From there, I'll run preliminary searches through the MLS system and put a handful of options in front of you. What do you love? What do you hate? How's the layout, the styling, the staterooms? Your feedback lets me fine-tune the search until we've narrowed it down to a few real contenders.

Then we go aboard. We'll walk the decks, inspect the holds, lie on the beds - and you'll try to picture yourself owning and loving each one. Some you'll pass on. Some will be fine. And then one will just feel right.

Once you're ready to move, I handle the negotiation, coordinate the hull and engine surveys with reputable surveyors, and manage the paperwork - registration, title, documentation, all of it. If you need financing, One Water Yacht Group has resources to help, or I'll work directly with your lender to make sure the deal funds smoothly.

And it doesn't stop at closing. If you need help getting familiar with your new boat, I'll connect you with a training captain who can walk you through safe operation, maintenance, and any insurance-required training.

Selling Assistance

Selling a yacht is a big deal. The stakes are high, the details matter, and getting it right from the start makes all the difference. That's where I come in.

I bring years of hands-on experience in the yacht brokerage industry, backed by One Water Yacht Group - one of the largest and most respected yacht sales organizations in the world. I know the market, I understand the technical side, and I'll make sure your vessel is priced accurately and positioned competitively from day one.

This isn't a cookie-cutter process. Every yacht and every owner's situation is different, and I take the time to understand yours. From tailored marketing strategies - professional photography, virtual tours, targeted advertising - to one-on-one consultations, your yacht gets the attention it deserves. Through One Water's extensive network of buyers, brokers, and industry contacts, your listing reaches the right people. More exposure means more opportunities and faster results.

From coordinating showings and handling the paperwork to negotiating with serious buyers, I'm with you at every step. My goal is simple: make the process as smooth as possible so you can focus on what comes next.

If you're thinking about selling, let's have a conversation. No pressure - just honest, straightforward guidance from someone who's been on both sides of the deal.
