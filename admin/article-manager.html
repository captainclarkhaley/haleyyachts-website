Article Manager

How to use:
(1) Select the article type.
(2) Upload your article as a Word document (.docx). The content will be extracted and converted automatically.
(3) Upload a hero image and any additional images for the article.
(4) Fill in the title, excerpt, and any category-specific fields.
(5) Click Preview to review, then Publish to go live.

First-time setup: The first time you publish, your browser will ask you to select the site's root folder. Select the folder that contains index.html, articles/, css/, etc.
