About Clark

Clark Haley's Biography

Clark Haley brings a unique blend of hands-on experience, deep industry knowledge, and entrepreneurial success to his role as a licensed Florida yacht broker. For over a decade, Clark and his wife Michelle cruised extensively aboard their Nordhavn 55, ROAM, covering more than 50,000 nautical miles. Their life at sea has taken them through countless adventures, equipping Clark with a rich understanding of yachts, systems, and the cruising lifestyle that few can match.

A lifelong mariner, Clark grew up sailing and fishing along the Texas coast. His family owned and operated a marine center that included a dive shop, sporting goods store, and yacht maintenance facility - an environment where he gained practical experience in vessel upkeep, systems management, and repair. He holds a U.S. Coast Guard 100GT Master Captain's License and has captained a wide range of vessels, from sailing dinghies to large sail and motor yachts.

After attending Texas A&M University, Clark went on to work in and manage several yacht sales operations in Texas and Maryland. He later channeled his entrepreneurial drive into launching and building a successful software company with offices across multiple states. Following the sale of the company, Clark and Michelle set out to embrace the cruising lifestyle full-time.

Now based in Jupiter, Florida, Clark has returned to his passion for yachting - this time helping others pursue their dreams on the water. Whether you're looking to purchase the ideal yacht or find the right buyer for your current vessel, Clark's depth of experience, technical insight, and genuine enthusiasm make him an exceptional partner in your journey.
