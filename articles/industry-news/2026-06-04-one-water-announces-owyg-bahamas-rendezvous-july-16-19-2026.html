One Water Announces "OWYG Bahamas Rendezvous" July 16-19, 2026

By Clark Haley · June 4, 2026 · 1 min read

Escape to the Bahamas with OneWater Yacht Group for an elevated owner experience designed around the boating lifestyle

The annual Summer Rendezvous will bring together owners and guests aboard Sunseeker, Riviera, Absolute, and HCB yachts for four unforgettable days of cruising, dockside hospitality, beach club experiences, island excursions, and curated social events.

Set against the backdrop of crystal-clear Bahamian waters, the Rendezvous is an opportunity to connect with fellow owners, experience the strength of the OneWater community, and enjoy a relaxed yet refined atmosphere both on and off the water. From welcome receptions and waterfront dining to beach gatherings and open-water adventures, every detail is designed to create a memorable owner experience.
