WARRANTY OF TITLE

WHEREAS, James Clark Haley (Seller), contracted to sell to Alpha Bravo Charley, LLC (Buyer), the vessel currently named “ROAM,” Coast Guard Number 1146516, and its inventory (collectively, the “Vessel”); and

WHEREAS, Buyer demanded Seller and Principal make certain representations and warranties regarding the Vessel’s Title and lien free status;

NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the premises, and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, Seller and Principal, jointly and severally, represent and warrant to Buyer the following:

  1. The Vessel is currently and validly reregistered with the U. S. Coast Guard. The Vessel’s ownership is not recorded in any other registry or system in the world.
  2. No individual or entity is owed for any outstanding services, materials, etc. rendered to, or for the benefit of, the Vessel; the Vessel is not obligated to any future claimants, including but not limited to, claims for future use or charter of the Vessel; there are no personal injury claims now outstanding, or possible of assertion, against the Vessel; and no judgement or decree has been entered against Seller which remains unsatisfied.
