The June issue of The Logbook, a monthly briefing from Clark Haley. This is the web version of the email that goes to friends and clients each month. Subscribe by getting in touch.

A Note from Clark

Friends,

If you have been thinking about taking your boat to the Bahamas, why not make this the summer you do it? The weather is predictable, the snorkeling and scuba diving are world class, and the crowds have headed north for the season. Anchorages that are normally packed will be nearly empty, and the locals will be glad you came. If going it alone makes you nervous, join us at the One Water Yacht Group (OWYG) Rendezvous, July 16-19 at the Grand Bahama Yacht Club in Freeport. Spend a few days with fellow boaters, then venture out from there. To help you get going, you will find several articles below on getting prepared for a fun, relaxing, and safe trip. See you out there.

Fair winds,
Clark
