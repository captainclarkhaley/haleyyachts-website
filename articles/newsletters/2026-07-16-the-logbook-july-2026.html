A Note from Clark

Friends,

Welcome to the July Logbook. The board is stacked. A brand-new 2024 Sunseeker Manhattan 68, still under full factory warranty, headlines a lineup that runs from the Riviera 545 "Fringe Benefits" and a 2023 Riviera 64 Sports Motor Yacht to a five-engine 2026 HCB 56 "Suenos" wearing a wall of quint Mercury 600s. Fair winds,
