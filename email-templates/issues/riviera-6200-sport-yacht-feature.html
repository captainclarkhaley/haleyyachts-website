Big-boat living in 62 feet: full-beam master, alfresco aft deck, twin Volvo Penta 900hp IPS. Step aboard.

A note from Clark Haley, Yacht Broker

 
 

World Premiere

 

Twenty years after Riviera reinvented the category with the original 3600 Sport Yacht, they have just pulled the cover off the boat the whole concept was building toward: the new Riviera 6200 Sport Yacht. I have spent a lot of time around this line, and I can tell you this one is special.

The headline is space. A new-generation hull lets the 6200 live far larger than her 62 feet, with a full-beam master suite, three or four staterooms, an all-seasons aft deck with its own alfresco galley, and a gourmet U-shaped galley below. This is single-level luxury living designed to actually be used, by family and friends, for real time aboard.

Step inside and the aft deck and saloon open into one grand entertaining space. Three sumptuous lounges, a fold-away timber dining table, a cinema-grade sound system and a TV that rises at the press of a button. Refrigeration, a wine cooler and a full bar station mean you provision once and stay out longer.

Out on the water she has the manners to match the looks. Twin Volvo Penta D13 IPS 1200 drives at 900hp deliver effortless blue-water performance with surprising fuel economy, plus joystick docking from three stations and assisted-docking technology that makes a 62-footer feel easy to handle short-handed.

There is a lot more to her, from the articulating swim platform that becomes your own private beach, to the tender garage, the palatial master ensuite, and Riviera's industry-leading warranties. I broke it all down in a full review.
